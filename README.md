\# Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard



\## Overview

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is an Excel-based data analysis project designed to monitor emergency room performance and patient activity. The dashboard provides a clear view of patient volume, waiting time, satisfaction levels, admissions, demographics, and department referrals.



\## Objectives

\- Monitor patient flow in the emergency room.

\- Analyze patient waiting time and satisfaction.

\- Track admission and non-admission rates.

\- Understand patient distribution by age group and gender.

\- Analyze department referral patterns.



\## Dashboard Highlights

\- Total Patient Analysis

\- Average Wait Time Tracking

\- Patient Satisfaction Monitoring

\- Admission Status Breakdown

\- Age Group Distribution

\- Gender-wise Analysis

\- On-time vs Delayed Attendance Analysis

\- Department Referral Analysis

\- Interactive Month and Year Filters



\## Tools Used

\- Microsoft Excel

\- Pivot Tables

\- Pivot Charts

\- Slicers

\- Dashboard Design and Formatting



\## Key Insights

\- A total of 479 patients visited the emergency room during the selected period.

\- The average patient wait time was 34.90 minutes.

\- The patient satisfaction score was 5.30.

\- More patients were attended on time than delayed.

\- Male and female patient counts were nearly balanced.

\- The 60–69 age group had the highest number of patients.

\- More than half of the patients were not admitted.

\- Most referrals were not assigned to a specific department, while General Practice received the highest number of referrals among departments.
